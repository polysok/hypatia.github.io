Frequently asked questions

How do I get started? +

On first launch, enter your name and your school level (collège, lycée, or higher education). Then configure at least one AI provider in Settings, open a conversation, and describe your exercise — the tutor will guide you step by step.

How do I set up an AI provider? +

hypatia lets you bring your own AI backend. The easiest free options:

  1. OpenRouter — create a free key at openrouter.ai, set the URL to https://openrouter.ai/api/v1, paste your key, and pick a model (free models are tagged).
  2. Groq — create a free key at console.groq.com, set the URL to https://api.groq.com/openai/v1, and enter a model name (e.g. llama-3.3-70b-versatile).
  3. Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+) — select it as your provider; it runs on-device with no configuration.
  4. Local model (Qwen 3.5) — download it once, then it runs entirely offline.
The app can’t connect to my local server +

iOS blocks plain http:// connections by default (App Transport Security). Local servers such as Ollama or LM Studio use HTTP, so you’ll need to expose them over HTTPS — via a reverse proxy (Caddy, nginx) or a tunnel (ngrok, Tailscale).

Tip: if you just want something that works out of the box, use OpenRouter or Groq, which are already HTTPS.
Is my data private? Where is it stored? +

Yes. hypatia is privacy-first:

  • Your conversations and notes are stored locally on your device and never synced to any cloud.
  • API keys are kept in the iOS Keychain (encrypted).
  • The app collects no analytics or telemetry.

Note: when you use a remote AI provider, your messages are sent to that provider to generate a reply, under their terms. Apple Intelligence and the local model keep everything on-device.

How do I change my school level? +

Open the Profile tab and change your level (collège, lycée, higher education). The tutor immediately adapts its vocabulary, pacing, and rigour to the new level.

Can I attach a photo of my exercise? +

Yes. You can attach images from the camera, your photo library, or the clipboard — they’re analyzed with AI vision or OCR. You can also paste a copied image into the Notes tab and run one-tap text recognition to capture the exercise as text.

Which languages are supported? +

French, English, Spanish, Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, Hindi, Indonesian, Tamil, Vietnamese, and Khmer.

Does hypatia cost anything? +

The app works with free AI models (via OpenRouter or Groq), Apple Intelligence, or a fully local model — so you can use it at no cost. If you connect a paid provider (Claude, GPT, etc.), you pay that provider directly for your usage.
